SCREENING INFORMATION

Candidtaes who has been offered provisional admission to are required to go through this screening procedure to qualify for the admission. The duration for this procedure is two(2) weeks, Thus it shold be noted that any candidate that fails to complete the screen process within the said stipulated period would be deemed to have rejected the offer of admission and therefore the offer would lapse.


Pacific Coast Screening Requirments

The documents highlighted below are needed for this screening procedure

  • Evidence of payment of acceptance fees
  • Original JAMB admission letter
  • SSCE/GCE/NECO, etc. Statement of result
  • Letter of refrence from a reputable Clergyman/Imam/Lawyer/Senior civil Servant
  • Birth certificate/Sworn Decleration of age
  • Local government of origin or State of origin
  • Passport Photograph wth red background
  • Original WAEC, GCE, NECO etc.(For candidates who obtained their entry qualification on or before 2018)

Failure to provide any of the above documents would make you not qualified for the admission. It should also be noted that the use of affidavit in lieu of State of result would not be accepted
